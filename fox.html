Faraaz's Zoo - Fox

Foxes are members of the Canidae family. Across various cultures, foxes are often portrayed as symbols of trickery and intelligence, usually being the clever protangonist or scheming villain of folktales. In Japanese folklore, foxes yokai, or kitsune, are depicted as mystical creatures capable of shape-shifting and possessing magical abilities. Foxes are found in various colors, such as orange, brown and white, as adaptations to their environment.
